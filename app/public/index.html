About Us

Oconee County Fire Resuce(OCFR) us a mostly volunteer organization based in Oconee County, GA. OCFR is a full-spectrum life safety agency protecting more than four million people who live, work and play in Oconee County.
The OCFR is a collective of 3,246 uniformed fire personnel and 353 professional rescue personnel dedicated to your safety.
